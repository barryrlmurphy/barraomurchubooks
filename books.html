BORU

A Novel by Barra Ó Murchú

In tenth-century Ireland, a young warrior rises from the ashes of his murdered family to challenge the Viking invaders and the fractured kings who let them reign. Brian Boru's journey from outlaw to High King is a story of ambition, sacrifice, and the terrible price of unity.

From the windswept cliffs of Clare to the blood-soaked battlefield of Clontarf, BORU brings medieval Ireland to vivid life—a world of treacherous alliances, ancient rivalries, and the relentless pursuit of an impossible dream.
